Sancha is a brand that provides various flavors of fruit teas that are made from a combination of fresh fruits and teas.

Each flavor has its own uniqueness, and our goal is to bring new and unique flavors to customers. Pre-made products allow customers to enjoy drinks anywhere without having to consider the steps of finding boiling water and mixing it with fruits. Sugar-free products can prevent customers from worrying that the drink is too sweet. The sweetness of the drink comes from the added fruit.

This is a packaging and branding project with goals to design a drink branding with brand name, brand mark, and three separate flavor bottle designs and create mockups for them.
